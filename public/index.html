Our customers don't know what bad Chinese food taste like

Welcome to Mei Mei Chinese restaurant, a family-owned business that has proudly served the people of New Jersey delicious Chinese food for more than 40 years! Not much has changed since our first days of business. Our recipes, the fresh ingredients we use and even location are still the same, which is why we believe our customers always come back for more.

Stop by and try our popular Egg rolls, General tso chicken, Special lunch platters, and lots more! We look forward to taking your order and seeing you at Mei Mei.
